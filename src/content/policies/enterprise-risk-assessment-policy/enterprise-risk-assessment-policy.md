# Enterprise Risk Assessment Policy

## 1. Purpose
Define a consistent method to identify, evaluate, prioritize, and treat risks affecting repository security and reliability.

## 2. Scope
Includes technical, operational, compliance, vendor, and process risks related to repository operations and delivery pipelines.

## 3. Risk Assessment Process
1. Identify risk scenario and impacted assets.
2. Evaluate likelihood and impact.
3. Assign overall risk rating.
4. Define treatment strategy and owner.
5. Track progress and reassess periodically.

## 4. Risk Treatment Options
* Mitigate: implement controls to reduce likelihood/impact.
* Transfer: shift risk through contractual or insurance mechanisms.
* Avoid: discontinue risky activity.
* Accept: formally acknowledge residual risk with expiry.

## 5. Risk Rating Guidance
Likelihood | Impact | Rating
--- | --- | ---
Low | Low | Low
Medium | Medium | Medium
High | High | High/Critical

Detailed scoring models may be maintained in supporting standards.

## 6. Risk Register Requirements
Each record must contain:
* Risk description and trigger.
* Affected assets and business impact.
* Owner and target remediation date.
* Current status and residual risk.

## 7. Review Cadence
* High/critical risks: review at least monthly.
* Medium risks: review quarterly.
* Low risks: review semi-annually.

## 8. Escalation
* Overdue high/critical risk treatments must be escalated to governance owners.
* Risk acceptance beyond defined thresholds requires leadership approval.
